Strengthen your mind and get your work done more efficiently!

We want you to be as mentally strong as you can be. Here we will teach you about computational thinking, how it can benefit you, and its use in our daily lives.

 

We plan to help you improve at different mental skills.

We want to encourage you to think clearly about any problem you may have and approach it in a systematic way. We want you to be able to develop a solution in such a way that it can be simple enough for another person or a computer to be able to execute it. These skills can assist you in tackling any problem that you may encounter. As long as you practice and take the steps that you believe are correct, your decision-making will be exceptional.

Critical Thinking

Computational thinking promotes critical thinking. You need to assess the information given to you, break it down and be able to understand the complexities behind it. The deeper you get into it, the more you'll understand and the easier you'll be able to mimic the process in future problems.

Problem Solving

The idea of computational thinking revolves around problem solving. You need to solve a problem, but you do so in a way that makes the problem easier, which then makes your path to the solution more simple as well. Your solution will be easy enough for anybody else to understand, even a computer.

Pattern Recognition

Through the process of compuational thinking, once you've broken everything down you are able to slowly identify any patterns that may be present in the problem. After you continue to recognize patterns you can easily transfer this skill to other things that you may experience in your life.
